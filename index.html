Projects Undertaken

  • As a part of my undergraduate B.Tech. project I worked with Prof. K.K. Shukla, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT BHU on Link Prediction Algorithms which involved predicting association/friendship in social networking sites. We compared various algorithms on the basis of performance metrics like accuracy, precision, specificity and sensitivity. Taking a step ahead we passed the results obtained by weak learners as parameters to an Artificial Neural Network wherein the weights were learnt by back propagation method which improved the sensitivity and the accuracy was comparable.

The design of mock-ups to final launch and working on both front-end and middle tier was indeed a fruitful experience.

  • The project on Online Auction for Used Assets in Information Technology Services, Tata Steel exposed me to MSSQL. The development work was done using Visual Basics as Backend and CSS as frontend.
